## Notable connections worth surfacing

A handful of the credits are worth highlighting because they connect
to wider Star Trek production history or to the team's prior work:

- **Patrick Stewart** voices Capt. Jean-Luc Picard, and **Brent Spiner**
  voices Cmdr. Data — the actors reprise their *Star Trek: The Next
  Generation* roles for their in-universe appearances during the
  campaign. (Picard commands the *Enterprise-E* on patrol of the
  Maelstrom; Data is co-author of the *Sovereign* technical notes.
  See [`characters/supporting-cast.md`](characters/supporting-cast.md).)
- **D.C. Fontana** — Story Editing & Dialogue. Fontana was a senior
  writer on *Star Trek: The Original Series* and *The Next Generation*;
  her involvement is a direct line to the Roddenberry-era writing room.
- **Derek Chester** — Story Editing & Dialogue.
- **Lawrence Holland** — Creative Director, Totally Games. Holland's
  team had previously made the *Star Wars: X-Wing*, *TIE Fighter*,
  *X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter* and *X-Wing Alliance* space-combat games
  (the manual's foreword calls them out). The combat-feel choices in
  Bridge Commander descend directly from that lineage.
- **Ron Surma** — Casting Director. Surma was the long-running casting
  director for the live-action Star Trek series of the period.
- **Gene Roddenberry** — Very Special Thanks.
