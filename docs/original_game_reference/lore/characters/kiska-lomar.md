## Personality

The opening fiction (p. 4) gives her a single defining moment:

> *"'We have arrived in the Vesuvi system, Captain,' Ensign Kiska
> reported with exuberance. It was unusual to find this attitude in
> most Bajorans, but her youth belied resentment of her people's
> struggle with the Cardassians. She was green, but very bright and
> extremely eager."*

Capt. Wright's review (1/25/2378):

> *"Ensign Kiska's six-month review indicates her performance has
> been highly satisfactory. Her skill and intelligence as a helm
> officer have been apparent from her first day aboard, and I find
> her a valuable addition to the Bridge Command rotation."*

> *"While her professional ability is unquestioned, I do feel her
> strong resentment of Cardassians will be a weakness unless she
> takes steps to overcome it. I have counseled her on the matter,
> and I understand the experience underlying it. I believe Ensign
> Kiska has made progress in dealing with this personal bias and
> that it will not adversely affect her advancement as a Starfleet
> officer. Her relationships with superior officers are excellent,
> and she has earned high marks in all areas."*

Lt. Felix Savali's peer evaluation (1/22/2378, attached to Wright's
review):

> *"I have worked with Ensign Kiska since her assignment to the
> Dauntless, directly after her graduation from the Academy. Her
> comprehension of communications procedure and operations is
> excellent. She knows the helm station well and exhibits strong
> skills and capabilities in ship maneuvering."*

> *"It is essential that Helm and Tactical work together, therefore
> Ensign Kiska and I have had many 'skull sessions' on that subject.
> She has an excellent grasp of combat maneuvers and an intuitive
> understanding of tactics. In my opinion, she should consider a
> transfer to Tactical down the line, but that will be a decision
> for her to make."*

**Reading these together:** Kiska is the *talented rookie*. Top of
her Academy class, 4th of 332, with a scholarship in academic honour
society membership. Wright assigned her a recommendation for
**promotion to second senior helm officer** — and *Felix* recommended
she eventually transfer to Tactical. Both senior officers see leader
in her.

The dramatic counterweight: she is **Bajoran**, with explicit, on-record
**resentment of Cardassians** rooted in her people's history. Wright
counselled her on it; she's reportedly making progress; the manual
flags it as her weakness — and the campaign is set on the
Cardassian frontier.

## In-game role

LoMar is the Conn. She drives the ship and runs comms.

- *Hail* — opens a list of nearby ships / colonies / bases for
  comms (and exposes friendly-ship orders when one is given to the
  player).
- *Set Course* → *Warp* — interstellar / inner-system transit (subject
  to the regulation that no warp goes directly to inner planets).
- *Orbit Planet* — establish planetary orbit.
- *Nav Points* — go to a plotted nav coordinate.
- *Intercept* — close on the current target (auto-warps if > 50 km).
- *All Stop*.
- *Dock* — only when in range of Starbase 12.

For her menu structure see
[`../../ui/bridge-mode.md`](../../ui/bridge-mode.md#helm-lomar-menu--f1).
