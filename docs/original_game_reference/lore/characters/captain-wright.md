# Captain Robert Wright

> Commanding Officer, USS *Dauntless* NCC-71879 (2372 – 2378-06-10).
> Killed in action at the Vesuvi incident.

The *Dauntless*'s captain immediately before the campaign begins. The
opening cinematic shows him handing the conn to the player and
shuttling down to the Vesuvi 3 colony, where he is killed.

## Personal description

From the manual's *Introduction* (p. 2):

> "Captain Wright was a veteran of three major conflicts — the war
> with the Cardassians, the fight against the Borg and the Dominion
> War. His service record was impressive, which is why he earned
> command of the Dauntless, the ship nicknamed within the ranks of
> Starfleet as 'The Seeing Eye.' Wright had experience, which showed in
> his graying hair and the chiseled lines in his face."
>
> "A tall man, he had a commanding presence. His confidence was
> tempered only by his humility, as evidenced by the way he always
> gave praise to his crew before himself. He was considered a just
> commander to serve under. In all that he was and in his every
> action, he had earned the respect of every crew he commanded."

## Career arc

- **Three major conflicts**: Cardassian War, Battle against the Borg,
  Dominion War.
- **2372** — assumed command of *Dauntless* from Capt. Leland Bell
  (after the ship completed its first major refit at the Utopia
  Planitia / similar yard).
- **Dominion War** — fought with elements of the **Seventh Fleet** at:
  - Battle of Tyris (where *Dauntless* earned her **2nd Unit Citation**
    by joining USS *Lionheart* in protecting two damaged *Akira*-class
    starships from an overwhelming Cardassian force).
  - Second Battle for Deep Space Nine.
  - Battle at Goralis.
  - The **Final Battle** — *Dauntless* earned her **3rd Unit
    Citation** while taking heavy damage, providing cover for the
    flagship and fending off four Cardassian suicide attacks.
- Post-war: routine fleet duties; *Dauntless* assigned to **Starbase 12**
  near the Maelstrom.

## Final mission (the campaign opener)

Manual's *Introduction* (pp. 2–5), narrated in the third person:

1. *Dauntless* completes a baryon sweep at the **Remmler array** at
   Arkaria base — needed maintenance after the Dominion War.
2. Wright receives orders from Adm. Liu: a routine resupply run to the
   **Vesuvi system**, plus an investigation of the system star's "odd
   behaviour" inconclusively reported by Vesuvi 3 science teams.
3. Wright remarks to his XO that the trip should be "fun" for **Miguel**
   (the Science Officer); Vesuvi is in the Maelstrom.
4. Wright preps a **personal package** — a blue bottle of **Romulan
   Ale** sent by his daughter Susan as a gift for his son-in-law,
   Chief Serson, on Vesuvi 3. He packs it discreetly because Romulan
   Ale is a banned intoxicant on Federation colonies; he asks the XO
   to omit any reference to it from the official log. *(Note: the
   manual states Romulan Ale "cannot be transported down" — Wright
   takes the shuttle.)*
5. Wright tells the XO he just finished writing the player's review
   and intends to discuss it on his return.
6. Hands the conn to the XO: *"Commander, you have the Conn. Shuttle
   Bay Two."*
7. Takes the shuttlecraft *Icarus* down to the Draegos colony.
8. Vesuvi event occurs. Wright is killed.

## Excerpt from Adm. Liu's letter of condolence

To: Acting Captain and Crew, U.S.S. *Dauntless* — DATE: 6/17/2378

> "It is with great regret that Starfleet received notice of Captain
> Wright's death in the Vesuvi incident one week ago. Captain Wright's
> death is a great loss to his family, to Starfleet and, without
> doubt, to you as his crew."
>
> "Finally, I wish to extend my personal condolences to the Dauntless
> and her crew. I have known Captain Wright for thirty years. In his
> personal and his professional life, he has always represented the
> epitome of what a Starfleet commanding officer should be. His death
> is a tremendous loss to all of us who knew him — and to those who
> now will never have that opportunity."
>
> — Alice Liu, Admiral Commanding, Starbase 12.

The Academy General Scholarship Fund accepts condolence credits in
his memory at his daughter Susan's request.

## Why he matters in gameplay

- His death is the **inciting event** of the campaign. The save-file
  identity of the player is created against a *Dauntless* without him.
- Adm. Liu's reaction to the player's command decisions is shaped by
  her grief and her thirty-year friendship with Wright.
- The Vesuvi incident's anomalous nature — and the suspicion that
  Wright's death wasn't natural — drives the early plot.
