# Stations

Static facilities the manual catalogues. Includes Federation
starbases / outposts / drydocks / Regula-series facilities, and
Cardassian starbase / station / outpost classes.

> The shield-rating six-tuple ordering is the same as ships:
> *Fore / Aft / Dorsal / Ventral / Port / Starboard*.

---

## Federation Starbase

The largest Federation orbital facility class.

### Background

- Gargantuan orbital space stations operated by Starfleet for **strategic
  command, service, supply, scientific research, and exploration**.
- Over **500 Federation Starbases** are located throughout Federation
  space, Federation territories, and beyond.

### Starbase 12 (the player's home base)

- Orbits **New Holland** along the Federation/Cardassian border, near
  the Maelstrom.
- 2364 — Evacuated during an alien attempt to infiltrate Starfleet.
- 2366 — *Enterprise-D* was scheduled here for maintenance.
- Currently commanded by **Adm. Alice Liu**.
- Wright's memorial service was held at this station 72 hours after
  the Vesuvi incident — chapel + ship review + wreath-casting.
- Susan Wright-Serson is stationed here.

### Specifications

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Displacement | 236,642,306 metric tons |
| Diameter | 4,600 m |
| Height (overall) | 6,950 m |
| Decks | 1,200 |
| Complement | 15,000 Starfleet; 20,000 to 35,000 civilian; 6,625 transients |
| Phasers | 8× RIM-12C Independent Twin Mount Phaser Emplacements |
| Shields | High capacity shield system, ~75,600,000 Terajoules |
| Hull rating | 220 |

> In gameplay terms: by far the **most defensively rated structure** in
> the catalogue. Players cannot meaningfully threaten it with the
> *Dauntless*'s firepower alone.

## Federation Outpost

Smaller facility class. Modern outposts are based on the Regula
starbase design but significantly larger and more versatile than
earlier space facilities.

Two variants:

- **Scientific** — Starbase 173 design.
- **Combat-orientated** — first introduced with Starbase 375.

Both variants provide starship support and deep-space scientific
research, plus significant defensive capabilities of their own.

### Maelstrom-active examples

- **Haven Facility** — orbits Haven (Vesuvi 6); serves commercial
  shipping and provides limited starship support.
- **Geki Station** — orbits Vesuvi 5; **suffered severe damage during
  the Vesuvi event and has since been abandoned**.

## Regula Series Space Facility

Originally designed for long-term scientific research projects in
isolated regions. Used by Starfleet, commercial, and civilian research
organisations. Starfleet maintains very few of these; those it does
are mostly in outlying sectors.

### Maelstrom-active examples

- **Soho Station** — orbits Tau Ceti Prime; provides starship support
  and serves as ops centre for three Starfleet drydock installations
  (where the *Nightingale* and *Dauntless* have been refitted).
- **Biranu Station** — Biranu System, orbiting Osa / Biranu 2.
  Established for starship support and resupply; also used for
  scientific and commercial conferences.
- **Savoy Station** — Savoy System, orbiting Savoy 1. Used for stellar
  research and starship support; equipped to host diplomatic summit
  meetings.

### Specifications

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Displacement | 200,000 metric tons |
| Diameter | 201 m |
| Height (overall) | 242 m |
| Decks | 28 |
| Complement | 10–50 personnel |
| Phasers | None |
| Shield ratings | 11 / 11 / 11 / 11 / 11 / 11 |
| Hull rating | 55 |

> **Unarmed**, but heavily shielded for their size — they're
> diplomatic / research / refit hubs, not combatants.

## Drydock

Large structures generally situated in planetary orbit. Used for the
service, repair, and construction of starships, commercial freighters,
or other space vessels.

### Background

- A drydock at the **San Francisco Yards** in Earth orbit was utilised
  for the *original Enterprise*'s post-five-year-mission refit under
  Capt. Kirk.
- The *Enterprise-B* was launched from another drydock orbiting Earth
  in 2293.

### Maelstrom-active

- **Three Starfleet drydock installations orbit Tau Ceti Prime** in
  the vicinity of Soho Station — where the *Nightingale* and
  *Dauntless* have been undergoing service and repairs.

### Specifications

| Field | Value |

| Displacement | 200,000 metric tons |
| Length / beam / height | 350 m / 190 m / 150 m |
| Complement | Variable |
| Phasers | None |
| Shield ratings | 6 / 6 / 6 / 6 / 6 / 6 |
| Hull rating | 20 |

## Cardassian Starbase

Massive orbital space stations — Cardassian equivalent of Federation
Starbase. Heavily armed.

### Architecture

- Operations Center.
- Promenade containing stores and service facilities.
- Outer docking ring.
- **Three massive docking towers** housing ore processing facilities
  (originally for refining uridium ore).

Standard complement: 300; capacity: 7,000 individuals.

### Known examples

- **Terok Nor** — now Deep Space Nine, operated by the Federation on
  behalf of the Bajoran people.
- **Empok Nor** — sister station to Terok Nor.
- **Litvok Nor** — *fabled* third sibling station. *"Cardassians favour
  the number three, and it has been widely speculated that a third
  station must exist. The location of this station remains a mystery,
  though Starfleet Intelligence has uncovered references to a Litvok
  Nor."* — manual.

### Maelstrom-active

- **Litvok Nor** — possibly located in or near the Maelstrom. Existence
  uncertain, location unknown. **Strong campaign-plot signal** — a
  classified war-era Cardassian asset hiding inside the most volatile
  stretch of space adjacent to the Federation border.

## Cardassian Station

Large-scale orbital facility, similar to Cardassian Starbases but **two
docking towers** instead of three and **no outer docking ring**.

- Towers still house ore-processing for uridium refining.
- Standard complement 250; capacity 2,500.
- Heavily armed; provide starship support and serve as drydocks for
  vessel maintenance / repair.

## Tactical implications (stations in general)

- All stations have **the longest effective phaser range** in the
  game (manual: "Stations generally have the greatest range of
  damage" — see [`../../ui/tactical-mode.md` § Combat behavioural rules](../../ui/tactical-mode.md#combat-behavioural-rules-manuals-combat-section)).
- Hull ratings are far higher than any ship — Federation Starbase 220,
  Cardassian Starbase 120 — and shield totals are likewise dwarfing.
- **Starbase support**: docking at Starbase 12 (LoMar's *Dock*)
  resupplies torpedoes and is the **only way** to repair systems on
  the *Destroyed Systems* list.
- *Defend the Starbase* multiplayer mode (see
  [`../../ui/quick-battle-and-multiplayer.md`](../../ui/quick-battle-and-multiplayer.md#defend-the-starbase))
  uses one of these station types as the protected target.
