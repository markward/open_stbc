# Klingon Ship Classes

The Klingon Defense Force operates two hull types in the manual's
catalogue.

| Class | Length | Hull rating | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| [B'rel-class Bird of Prey](#brel-class-bird-of-prey) | 88 m | 4 | Fast scout / light combatant |
| [Vor'cha](#vorcha-class-attack-cruiser) | 482 m | 18 | Heavy attack cruiser / command ship |

Both classes carry **cloaking devices** as standard.

---

## B'rel-class Bird of Prey

**Fast, light, manoeuvrable scout / light combatant.** The mainstay of
the modern Klingon Imperial Fleet.

### Lineage

A family of Klingon Birds of Prey going back to ~2285. Capable of
**atmospheric and warp-speed interstellar flight**. Variants include:

- The original **D-11**-class Bird of Prey.
- Its replacement, the **B'rel**-class scout.
- The larger **K'Vort**-class cruiser.

The **D-12** scout variant (developed in the 2350s) suffered defective
plasma coils in its cloaking device and was retired in favour of the
D-13 / B'rel.

Earlier D-12 hulls had a 37-crew complement; modern D-12 / B'rel
hulls require no more than 12 officers and crew.

In 2293, an **experimental D-12** prototype demonstrated **launching
photon torpedoes while cloaked** — used by Gen. Chang against
*Enterprise-A* in an unsuccessful attempt to disrupt the Khitomer Peace
Conference.

## Vor'cha-class Attack Cruiser

**Heavy cruiser** of the Klingon Defense Force. Among the largest and
most powerful vessels in the Imperial fleet (still surpassed by the
*Negh'Var* class).

### Background

- Originally built in a failed attempt to gain superiority over the
  Federation.
- Later proved to be a valuable command ship in the Dominion War.
- Klingon High Council leader **K'mpec** used a *Vor'cha* in 2367 on
  his final mission, entreating Capt. Picard to serve as Arbiter of
  Succession after his death.
- In 2372, a *Vor'cha* attacked the USS *Defiant* (under Capt. Sisko)
  while *Defiant* was en route to Cardassia Prime in an attempt to
  rescue Gul Dukat and the Detapa Council. The Klingon cruiser pursued
  *Defiant* all the way to Deep Space 9.

## Tactical implications (Klingon hulls)

- **Cloak on both classes** — the player ship cannot phaser-target a
  cloaked Klingon. Use sensor scans (Miguel/`F4`) and proximity to
  detect.
- The B'rel's **fore shield rating of 13** is high vs. its hull rating
  of 4 — Klingon doctrine: tough head-on, fragile elsewhere.
- The Vor'cha's **fore shield rating of 38** is the highest in the
  catalogue — these ships are designed to charge head-on.
