## Akira class

**Battlecruiser.** Designed to complement *Galaxy* and *Nebula* hulls,
freeing *Excelsior* and *Ambassador* classes from light combat duty.

### Background

- Pure combat orientation — a deviation from Starfleet's exploratory
  charter.
- Need for a highly manoeuvrable, well-armed and survivable battleship
  became clear with the onset of the Cardassian War.
- Several *Akira*-class vessels fought as part of the combined Alpha-
  Quadrant fleet that invaded Cardassian space in 2374.
- **Three** *Akira*-class ships were lost to Cardassian orbital weapons
  platforms in the **Chin'toka system**.
- USS *Thunderchild* NCC-63549 was an *Akira*-class.

### Maelstrom-active hulls

- **USS *Devore*** (Capt. Joshua Martin) — Vesuvi colonial defence.
- **USS *Geronimo*** (Capt. Gregory MacCray) — combat training, Savoy.

## Ambassador class

**Heavy cruiser**, commissioned 2325. Designed as a replacement for the
ageing *Excelsior*-class. Larger explorer-type vessel for longer-
duration missions; generally serves at the edge of Federation space at
the forefront of first-contact missions.

### Background

- Gradually being replaced by the larger *Nebula* and *Galaxy*-class
  starships.
- Hulls of class: USS *Adelphi* NCC-26849, USS *Excalibur* (second)
  NCC-26517, USS *Zhukov* NCC-26136, USS *Enterprise* NCC-1701-C
  (the fourth *Enterprise*).

### Maelstrom-active hulls

- **USS *Zhukov*** (Capt. Milus Verata) — diverted to the Tevron system
  for emergency escort.
- **USS *Excalibur*** (Capt. William Morrison) — ordered to Starbase 12
  for crew transfer / resupply.

## Galaxy class

**Federation's primary exploration vessel** as of 2378. Designed to
replace the *Ambassador*-class heavy cruiser. Among the most advanced
and most powerful vessels in Starfleet.

### Background

- Manufacturing cost is so high that **fewer than 15 hulls were ever
  built**.
- USS *Galaxy* NCC-70637 (lead ship) — commissioned 2357.
- USS *Yamato* NCC-71807 — second hull; destroyed 2365.
- **USS *Dauntless* NCC-71879** — third hull; commissioned 2365 (the
  player's ship — see [`uss-dauntless.md`](uss-dauntless.md)).
- USS *Enterprise-D* NCC-1701-D — launched 2363.
- Other Galaxy-class hulls: USS *Challenger* NCC-71099, USS *Odyssey*
  NCC-71832, USS *Venture* NCC-71854.

### Maelstrom-active hulls

- **USS *Dauntless*** (Capt. Robert Wright, then the player) —
  Vesuvi-system stellar research.
- **USS *Venture*** (Capt. Benjamin Dawson) — combat ops, Artrus
  system.
- **USS *San Francisco*** (Capt. Eina Zeiss) — combat ops, Artrus
  system.

## Nebula class

**Light cruiser** — the Federation's lower-cost Galaxy-alternative.

### Background

- Slightly smaller than *Galaxy* but shares many advanced components,
  systems, and design features.
- **Integrated** hull structure (single hull rather than separable
  saucer + engineering sections) — more cost-effective for universal
  production and deployment.
- True multi-role vessel: features a large **dorsal equipment module**
  alternately configurable for combat or research missions (advanced
  weapons platforms or sophisticated sensor packages).
- Hulls of class: USS *Phoenix* NCC-65420, USS *Sutherland* NCC-72015,
  USS *Bellerophon* NCC-62048, USS *Khitomer* NCC-71906.

### Maelstrom-active hulls

- **USS *Berkeley*** (Capt. Elizabeth Haley) — Vesuvi-system stellar
  research.
- **USS *Nightingale*** (Capt. Nandi Jadeja) — *hospital ship*
  assigned to Vesuvi system.
- **USS *Khitomer*** (Capt. Jae Yi) — combat ops under mission
  commander Jonathan Willis.

## Sovereign class

**Most advanced starship in Starfleet** as of 2373. Next-generation
heavy cruiser. ~700 m, 24 decks.

### Background

- USS *Sovereign* **NX-73811** is the prototype (currently being refit
  in the Vesuvi system — see [`uss-sovereign.md`](uss-sovereign.md)).
- USS *Enterprise-E* is the second hull, launched 2372 under
  Capt. Picard.
- Pioneered (or carried into service) regenerative shielding,
  ablative hull armour, bio-neural gel packs, phase-plasma torpedoes
  (research-only), and an enhanced deflector system. See
  [`../setting.md` § The Sovereign Project](../setting.md#the-sovereign-project-classified--captains-eyes-only)
  for the full Sovereign Project history.

### Maelstrom-active hulls

- **USS *Sovereign*** (temporary Capt. Robert Soto) — Vesuvi-system
  major refit.
- **USS *Enterprise*** (Capt. Jean-Luc Picard) — assigned routine
  patrol of the Maelstrom.
