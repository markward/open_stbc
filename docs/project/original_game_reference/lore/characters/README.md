# Characters

Bios for the named cast of *Star Trek: Bridge Commander*, drawn from
the manual's *Crew Records, USS Dauntless* (pp. 41–53), the *Letter of
Condolence* (p. 40), the *Sovereign Project* notes (pp. 53–58), and the
introductory fiction (pp. 2–5).

## The acting captain (the player)

The protagonist is the *Dauntless*'s **First Officer** at the start of
the campaign, who takes acting command after Wright's death in the
Vesuvi incident.

The manual deliberately leaves the First Officer **unnamed and
faceless** to allow the player to project themselves onto the role.
What we know:

- Six months serving as XO under Wright at the time of the incident.
- Wright's final personal words to them were: *"Commander, you have
  the Conn. Shuttle Bay Two."*
- Wright had just finished writing a glowing review for the player and
  intended to discuss it on his return: *"After six months' service
  aboard this vessel, the Commander has proven to be a most promising
  First Officer. I have no doubt more seasoning will see him on his way
  to his own starship command."*
- Player name and difficulty are set on the New Game screen (see
  [`../../ui/launch-and-main-menu.md`](../../ui/launch-and-main-menu.md#new-game-pane));
  these are the only customisable elements of the protagonist.

## Supporting cast

[`supporting-cast.md`](supporting-cast.md) covers the named characters
who appear in the manual fiction outside the *Dauntless* bridge crew:

- **Adm. Alice Liu** — CO, Starbase 12; Wright's friend of 30 years.
- **Lt. Susan Wright-Serson** — Wright's daughter, on Starbase 12.
- **Chief Serson** — Starfleet Corps of Engineers, Vesuvi 3 colony,
  Wright's son-in-law.
- **Lt. Nathan James** — Engineer at the Vesuvi 3 colony, Serson's
  long-time friend.
- **Jordan** — Communications officer at the Vesuvi 3 biosphere.
- **Capt. Leland Bell** — Wright's predecessor in command of the
  *Dauntless* (2365–2372).
- **Geordi LaForge** — Chief Engineer, USS *Enterprise* NCC-1701-E;
  corresponds with Brex about the *Sovereign* refit.
- **Lt. Cmdr. Data** — *Enterprise-E*; co-author of the *Sovereign*
  technical notes.
- **Capt. Jean-Luc Picard** — CO, *Enterprise-E*; assigned routine
  patrol of the Maelstrom.
- **Sarpon** — Master Prefect of Space Sciences, Vulcan Science
  Academy (Diaz's master's referee).
- **Capt. Thomas Wittbeck** (USS *Lionheart*), **Capt. Lorena Damon**
  (*Parsifal*), **Capt. Serchin Davas** (USS *Arapaho*), **Capt.
  Didier LaFleur** (USS *Baltic*), **Cmdr. Tornan Tresan** (USS
  *Mohawk*), **Capt. Delia Messina** (USS *Strongbow*) — past COs
  who wrote service-jacket evaluations for the *Dauntless* crew.

## How the game uses these characters

- All five bridge officers are addressable by their function-key menus
  (`F1`–`F5`) — see [`../../ui/bridge-mode.md`](../../ui/bridge-mode.md).
- *Report* on each officer's menu solicits their personality-driven
  advice, comments, or opinions about the current situation.
- Mission scripts may add **guests** (`F6`) — typically Liu or other
  named NPCs from the supporting-cast file.
- Hailing (LoMar's *Hail* menu) is the canonical contact path to ships
  / colonies / bases owned by named characters (e.g. *Contact
  Starfleet* opens a channel to Liu).
