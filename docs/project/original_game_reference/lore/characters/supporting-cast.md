# Supporting Cast

Named characters in the manual fiction outside the *Dauntless* bridge
crew. Some appear in the cinematic or as guests on the bridge (`F6`);
others exist only as authors of the service-jacket evaluations that
flesh out the bridge crew's career history.

---

## Federation officers (active)

### Admiral Alice Liu

**Role**: Commanding officer, **Starbase 12**.

- Friend of Capt. Robert Wright for thirty years (manual: she has
  "known Captain Wright for thirty years").
- Authored Wright's letter of condolence dated 6/17/2378 — the
  document that sets the tone for the player's first interactions with
  Starfleet command after the Vesuvi incident.
- Memorial service for Wright held at her station (chapel + ship
  review + wreath-casting at 1300/1400 starbase time).
- In the campaign, she is the destination of Saffi's *Contact
  Starfleet* on the bridge crew menu — the "voice of Starfleet Command"
  for the *Dauntless* throughout the storyline.

> Personal: she is openly grieving in her condolence letter ("the
> epitome of what a Starfleet commanding officer should be") — likely
> coloured in early-campaign briefings.

---

### Lieutenant Susan Wright-Serson

**Role**: Personnel officer at **Starbase 12** (rank stated as
Lieutenant; specific posting on starbase implied by Liu's letter).

- Daughter of Capt. Robert Wright.
- Wife of **Chief Serson** (Starfleet Corps of Engineers, Vesuvi 3).
- Sent Wright a bottle of **Romulan Ale** via the *Dauntless* run as a
  gift for her husband Serson — the gift Wright was carrying to the
  Vesuvi 3 colony at the time of his death.
- After the Vesuvi incident, she requests in lieu of synth-flowers
  that condolence credits go to the **Starfleet Academy General
  Scholarship Fund** in her father's memory.

She is doubly affected by the Vesuvi incident — she loses her father
*and* her husband (Serson was on Vesuvi 3 at the time).

---

### Captain Leland Bell (retired)

**Role**: First captain of the USS *Dauntless* (2365 – 2372).

- Commanded *Dauntless* from her commissioning in August 2365 through
  the ship's first major refit in 2372 (when Wright took over).
- Wrote the 7/23/2372 promotion-to-Lt. Cmdr. recommendation for Brex.
- Retired before the *Dauntless* entered the Dominion War-era refit.

---

### Captain Jean-Luc Picard

**Role**: Commanding officer, USS *Enterprise* NCC-1701-E.

- Listed in the manual's *Operational Ships and Facilities, Maelstrom*
  as **assigned routine patrol of the Maelstrom**. The *Enterprise-E*
  is therefore a unit operating in the player's region of space.

---

### Lt. Cmdr. Data

**Role**: *Enterprise-E* officer; co-author with Geordi LaForge of the
*Sovereign* technical operating notes referenced in the *Sovereign*
project file.

---

### Geordi LaForge

**Role**: Chief Engineer, USS *Enterprise* NCC-1701-E.

- Sends a personal letter to **Lt. Cmdr. Brex** congratulating him on
  the *Sovereign* refit assignment and providing detailed operational
  advice (warp-core intermix ratios, EPS-junction recommendations on
  Decks 10 and 15, shakedown tips).
- "Chief Brex, let me begin by saying 'congratulations on winning the
  prize.'"
- The two are "the only two engineers in Starfleet to call the
  Sovereign class our home" once the *Sovereign* refit completes.

## The Vesuvi 3 colony — engineers killed/affected

The opening fiction (pp. 3–4) names three colonists at the Draegos
biosphere on Vesuvi 3. All are present at ground zero of the Vesuvi
incident.

### Chief Serson

**Role**: Starfleet Corps of Engineers; Foreman, Draegos atmosphere-
processor build, Vesuvi 3.

- Married to Lt. Susan Wright-Serson; son-in-law of Capt. Robert
  Wright.
- Posted to Draegos for the past three months. Frustrated to be
  "routing ODN lines for an atmosphere generator" given his expertise
  in terraforming and his contributions to the *Prometheus* — he had
  hoped to be assigned to the *Sovereign* refit.
- Six months of work to go on the atmosphere processor at the time of
  the Vesuvi incident.
- Was about to receive the bottle of Romulan Ale his wife had sent via
  Capt. Wright (and the *Dauntless*) as a coping gift for the harsh
  conditions of the post.

### Lieutenant Nathan James

**Role**: Engineer assigned to Draegos with Serson.

- Long-time friend of Serson — the two have been on assignments
  together for six years.
- Sceptical-but-cheerful counterweight to Serson's grumbling.
- Hates Hyronalin radiation hypos ("makes me vomit") but takes them
  because Jordan reminds him to.

### Jordan

**Role**: Communications Officer inside the Vesuvi 3 biosphere
(presumably Starfleet, rank unspecified).

- Female. Speaks via the suit-intercom system to remind Serson and
  James to come in for their Hyronalin hypos when their safe time
  outside has passed.
- Friendly antagonist banter with Nathan James — calls him a "pigeon
  for poker night."

The fiction breaks off as the *Dauntless* is crossing into the Vesuvi
star's light. The Vesuvi event happens immediately after.

## Notes on usage in-game

- Mission-specific guests appear at the bridge **guest slot** (`F6`).
  The natural candidates from this list are Adm. Liu (briefings), and
  potentially Lt. Wright-Serson (mission-specific cutscene contact).
- *Hail* (LoMar's menu) is the canonical contact channel to any of the
  above ship COs when their vessels are nearby.
- *Contact Starfleet* (Saffi's menu) by default reaches Adm. Liu at
  Starbase 12.
- *Contact Engineering* (Saffi's menu) reaches Brex when he is at his
  Engineering station.
