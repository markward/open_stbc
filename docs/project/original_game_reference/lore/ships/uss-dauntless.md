## Service history

### Under Captain Leland Bell (2365–2372)

- 2365 — Joined the **Seventh Fleet** under Bell's command. Initial
  assignments were "milk runs."
- That changed when *Dauntless* fought a series of skirmishes against
  a flight of Romulan warbirds in a disputed area of the Alpha
  Quadrant. She held the area until other Starfleet vessels arrived.
  → **First Unit Citation**.
- During the unsettled period that followed, ran scouting and
  reconnaissance patrols. The repeated reconnaissance work earned her
  reputation and the nickname **"The Seeing Eye."**
- 2372 — Returned to space dock at the end of her seventh year for
  refit and equipment upgrades. Capt. Bell retired. **Capt. Robert
  Wright** became commanding officer.

### Under Captain Robert Wright (2372–2378)

Fought through the **Dominion War** with elements of the Seventh Fleet:

| Engagement | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Battle of Tyris | Joined USS *Lionheart* in protecting two severely damaged *Akira*-class starships from an overwhelming Cardassian force. **Earned 2nd Unit Citation** + battle honours. |
| Second Battle for Deep Space Nine | (Engaged.) |
| Battle at Goralis | (Engaged.) |
| Final Battle | While taking heavy damage, provided cover for the flagship and **fought off four suicide attacks by Cardassian vessels**. **Earned 3rd Unit Citation**. |

Post-war: returned to normal Fleet duties. Currently assigned to
**Starbase 12**, near the Maelstrom.

### Pre-campaign present-tense (early 2378)

- Just completed a **baryon sweep** at the **Remmler array, Arkaria
  base** — needed maintenance after the war.
- Bridge crew rosters now show: Wright (CO), the player (XO), Larsen
  (XO/transferred from *Lionheart*; see service-history note in
  [`../characters/saffi-larsen.md`](../characters/saffi-larsen.md)),
  LoMar (Helm, six months aboard since Academy), Felix (Tactical,
  recently transferred from *Strongbow*), Diaz (Science, two years
  aboard), Brex (Chief Engineer, aboard since at least 2370 across
  multiple commanders).
- Ordered to the **Vesuvi system** for a routine resupply run + stellar
  observation — the mission that opens the campaign.

### From the Vesuvi incident onward

- 6/10/2378 — **Captain Wright is killed** at Vesuvi.
- 6/17/2378 — Adm. Liu writes the official condolence to "Acting
  Captain and Crew, U.S.S. *Dauntless*" — the player.
- 72 hours after the incident — Memorial service at Starbase 12.

## Power and damage characteristics

The *Dauntless* uses the standard *Galaxy*-class power layout:

- **Warp Core** — primary power source.
- **Main Battery** + **Reserve Battery** — secondary reservoirs.
- Per-system Power Allocation Sliders for **Weapons**, **Engines**,
  **Sensor Array**, **Shield Generator** (Engineering / Brex's panel).
- **No cloak** (Treaty of Algeron; only available on faction ships in
  MP/QB).
- **No phased plasma torpedoes** (still in research).

Default loadout reflects the *Galaxy*-class spec:

- 8× Type X collimated phaser arrays.
- 6× Mk 80 direct-fire **photon** torpedo tubes (4 forward + 2 aft).
- CIDSS-3 primary force field & deflector control.
- Shields 13/9/9/9/9/9. Hull rating 15.

## Why "The Seeing Eye"

The nickname comes from her early assignments in scouting and
reconnaissance — long-range patrols where her sensor suite mattered
more than her firepower. The name has stuck. Lt. Cmdr. Diaz's continued
work on sensor reception patterns (his Vulcan Science Academy thesis)
extends the tradition.
