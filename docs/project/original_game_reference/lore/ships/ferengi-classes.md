# Ferengi Ship Classes

The Ferengi Alliance is represented by a single hull in the manual's
catalogue: the *D'Kora*-class Marauder.

| Class | Length | Hull rating | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| [D'Kora](#dkora-class-marauder) | 487 m | 10 | Trading vessel / capable warship |

---

## D'Kora-class Marauder

**Largest, most powerful ship used by the Ferengi Alliance.** Primarily
a trading vessel, but a sophisticated and capable warship.

### Background

- Some *Marauders* are equipped with powerful **plasma energy emitters**
  capable of disabling a *Galaxy*-class starship.
- All *D'Kora* hulls feature a high-power **ventral phaser beam**.
- First *D'Kora* encountered by Starfleet was in 2364, when *Enterprise-D*
  intercepted a Marauder in the vicinity of **Gamma Tauri IV**.

### Maelstrom-active

- **Marauder *Krayvis*** (Daimon **Praag**) — has been sighted in the
  Maelstrom.

## Tactical implications

- **No cloak.**
- **Ventral phaser beam + plasma emitters** are positioned to
  threaten ships from below and ahead of the Marauder; Federation
  doctrine is to attack from above (top shields face Ferengi guns
  tend to be weaker).
- **Symmetric port/starboard shielding (5/5)** is the lowest-rated
  pair of faces — flanking is still effective.
- The **plasma emitters' anti-Galaxy capability** makes a single
  Marauder a credible threat to a *Dauntless*-class hull.
- The class doesn't appear in the manual's torpedo loadout list —
  effectively a beam-only ship, consistent with the trader-with-teeth
  profile.

---

## Cultural / diplomatic notes

- The Ferengi Alliance is **not a treaty-restricted power** — the
  manual's geopolitical context (Cardassian, Breen, Gorn restrictions)
  doesn't apply.
- *Marauders* in the Maelstrom can be opportunistic — looking for
  trade angles around the Vesuvi-system mining colonies, or
  privateering wrecks left by the Vesuvi event.
