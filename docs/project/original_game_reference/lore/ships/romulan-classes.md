# Romulan Ship Classes

The Romulan Star Empire is represented in the manual's class catalogue
by a single, very powerful hull: the *D'Deridex*-class warbird.

| Class | Length | Hull rating | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| [D'Deridex](#dderidex-class-warbird) | 1,042 m | 24 | Heavy warbird |

---

## D'Deridex-class Warbird

**Incredibly massive and powerful.** Nearly twice the size and length
of a Galaxy-class starship.

### Background

- First encountered by Starfleet in 2364, when the Romulans violated
  the Neutral Zone in response to Borg attacks.
- Originally classified by Starfleet as **B-Type Warbirds**.
- **Powered by an artificial quantum singularity** — not an antimatter
  reactor — driving the warp system.
- Starfleet records indicate **a Romulan Warbird was destroyed in 2369
  when its power core was infested by extra-dimensional life-forms**.

### Combat doctrine

Romulan Warbirds:

- Have **greater firepower than vessels of similar class**.
- Are **slightly less manoeuvrable**.
- Have a **lower sustainable warp speed**.
- **Favour frontal attacks** because of their limited manoeuvrability
  and the **mostly forward positioning of their armaments**.

### Maelstrom-active

- **I.R.W. *Soryak*** (Capt. Torenn) — sighted in the Tevron and Beol
  systems.
- **I.R.W. *Chairo*** (Capt. Terrik) — sighted in the Belaruz system.

## Tactical implications

- **Symmetric shield ratings** (8 on every face) plus **hull rating
  24** make the Warbird the most resilient ship-class in the catalogue
  by a wide margin.
- **Forward-favoured armaments** mean the Warbird is **far less
  threatening on its sides and rear** — the doctrinal counter is to
  flank.
- **Cloak** — same caveats as Klingon hulls: use sensor scans and
  proximity heuristics.
- Romulan-faction ships in the campaign are ostensibly Federation
  *allies* under the post-Dominion-War coalition, but accusations of
  hidden military forces are part of the geopolitical climate (see
  [`../setting.md`](../setting.md)).
